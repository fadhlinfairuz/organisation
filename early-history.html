Early History of Kelantan

Flag of Kelantan

The Kelantan flag symbolises the state‚Äôs Islamic identity and royal sovereignty. The red background represents courage and strength, while the white crescent and star reflect Islam as the guiding principle of governance and society in Kelantan.

Map of Kelantan

Kelantan is located on the northeastern coast of Peninsular Malaysia. Its geographical position facilitated early trade, cultural exchange, and political interaction with Siam and neighbouring Malay states, shaping its early historical development.

Selamat Sultan Song

Selamat Sultan is the official song of the state of Kelantan. The melody for this song was composed by the late Haji Mohamed bin Mohamed Saaid on 5 July 1927, while the lyrics were composed by Tengku Mahmood Mahyideen in 1930.
